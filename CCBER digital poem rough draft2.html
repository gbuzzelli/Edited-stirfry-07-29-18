1. Ecosystem Restoration: Enhance wetland and associated upland habitats characteristic of the Devereux Slough ecosystem. To do so will require expansion of wetland area, improved hydrological connectivity, control of invasive non-native species, re-introduction of native species, enhancement of habitats for threatened and endangered species, and improving resiliency of ecosystem structure and function. 2. Provide Social Values: Maintain open space and develop opportunities for passive recreation, and research, and educational use that are compatible with the environmentally sensitive resources of the area.
